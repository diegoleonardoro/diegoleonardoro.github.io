Landmark's Century Centre Cinema

The theatre has been located in 2828 North Clark Street at Diversey since 2001. It is Chicago's first all-stadium seating theatre showcasing independent and foreign language films. Surrounded by award-winning restaurants. It attracts an audience that appreciates good food and art.

Context:

Since its opening in 2001, Landmark's Century Centre Cinema has sucessfully catered to and audience that appeciates foreign, independent and indie movies. Due to its success, in the Spring of 2015, the theatre was extensively renovated, and in 2021, as society moves from the global pandemic, they are looking to open a new location in Chicago.

Challenge:

Chicago has a good supply of theatres that satisfy the demand for independent and foreing movies. They are scattered throughout they city, and they serve a unique audience. Due to one and a half successful decade with a solid and growing customer base, Landmark's Theatre is now looking for an untapped location for a new theatre.

To do so, it is necessary to understand the area where Landmark's Century Centre Cinema is located to see if it has any unique characteristics.
